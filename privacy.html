Privacy

Local-first by design.

Folines is being built as a standalone Windows app. The desktop app is designed to store wallpaper settings locally and does not require an account for core use.

This early website does not include analytics, ad tracking, or third-party tracking pixels. If you email Folines, your message is used only to respond to your request.

As the product evolves, this page will be updated before any paid account, telemetry, or cloud feature is introduced.
