Product

Not a video wallpaper. A text surface with intent.

Screen-aware: assign themes, quote categories, pacing, and color logic per monitor.

Self-healing: recover from display changes, sleep, unlock events, and blank wallpaper states.

Private by default: local-first configuration, no telemetry requirement, no account required for the desktop app.
